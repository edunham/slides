Implicature, or, how not to diversity. 

im·pli·ca·ture
ˈimplikəCHər/
noun
the action of implying a meaning beyond the literal sense of what is
explicitly stated, e.g., saying the frame is nice and implying I don't like
the picture in it.
a meaning so implied.
plural noun: implicatures
